  Neighborhoods & Market

  What are the most popular neighborhoods in Austin right now?  
  \- South Congress / Zilker: Walkable, trendy, great for young  
  professionals. Median home price \~$750K.  
  \- East Austin: Vibrant arts scene, rapid appreciation. Median  
  \~$600K.  
  \- Hyde Park / North Loop: Historic bungalows, UT proximity.  
  Median \~$550K.  
  \- Circle C Ranch: Family-friendly suburbs, great schools. Median  
  \~$650K.  
  \- Mueller: Master-planned community near downtown. Median \~$700K.  
  \- Round Rock / Cedar Park: More affordable, growing rapidly.  
  Medians $400–550K.  
  \- Buda / Kyle: Best value in the metro. Medians $350–450K.

  Is it a buyer's or seller's market right now?  
  Austin is currently a balanced-to-slightly-buyer-friendly market.  
   Inventory has increased from 2022 lows, giving buyers more  
  negotiating power than in previous years. Interest rates remain  
  elevated but prices have stabilized.

  What is the average home price in Austin?  
  The median sale price in Austin metro is approximately $485,000  
  as of 2025\. Downtown condos and South Austin single-family homes  
  typically run $550K–$1.2M. Suburban areas range from $350K–$600K.

  Financing & Mortgage

  Do you have mortgage partners?  
  Yes. We work closely with several Austin-area lenders who offer  
  competitive rates. We can refer you based on your situation  
  (first-time buyer, VA, jumbo, investor, etc.).

  What down payment do I need?  
  Conventional loan: 3–20% down. FHA: 3.5% down (requires mortgage  
  insurance). VA loan: 0% down for eligible veterans. Jumbo:  
  typically 10–20%.

  What credit score do I need to buy a home?  
  Minimum 580 for FHA, 620 for conventional. Better rates start at  
  700+.
