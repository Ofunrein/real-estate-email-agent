# Research — Iris motion replacement

## Brief

- Viewer: real-estate team leader or operations director.
- Primary action: understand how Iris turns email into controlled operational work.
- Anti-goals: consumer AI spectacle, invented automation, generic SaaS promo.

## Reference motion vocabulary

Attached frames use hard black negative space, giant condensed type, brief glitch/data texture, short declarative cards, deep UI stacks, and camera travel through floating panels. Pacing alternates 1–2 second kinetic punctuation with 5–8 second product proof. Depth comes from foreground blur, scale separation, overlapping monitors, and lateral parallax. Typography works as scene architecture. Transitions use match-scale, rapid defocus, occlusion, and data-field dissolves rather than crossfade-only edits.

Transfer: layered UI planes, decisive scale, terminal texture, camera moves, rapid bridge cards. Reject: exact monochrome/purple palette, orb, arrows, logo, copy, and sequence.

## Non-web references

1. Film title systems: oversized type becomes spatial object; 64–168px scale and sharp cuts.
2. Broadcast control rooms: dense status labels and layered monitors; 1px borders, mono labels, asymmetric composition.

## Default-agent blocks

- Block `#F9FAFB`; use near-black green.
- Block Inter; use Avenir Next and condensed face.
- Block blue-500; use grounded clay.
- Block 8px cards; cap at 6px.
- Block default soft shadow; use border-first panels and rare deep camera shadow.
- Block centered 1200px grid; use full-bleed cinematic staging.
